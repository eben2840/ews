Financial & Life Insurance with EWS

Term life insurance provides coverage for a specific period of time, or "term" of years. If the insured person dies within the "term" of the policy and the policy is still in force (active), then the death benefit is paid out to the beneficiary.

This type of insurance typically allows clients to initially purchase more insurance coverage for less money (premium) than other kinds of life insurance. EWS's "Buy Term and Invest the Difference" philosophy encourages clients to take this "difference" in premium and invest it for the future.

Term life insurance is generally much more affordable in the initial years to provide the right amount of coverage you really need for your family.
